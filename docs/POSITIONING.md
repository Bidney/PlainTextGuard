# Positioning Notes

PlainText Guard should be described as a formatting, compliance, and plain-text hygiene tool.

Use language like:

- Copy ASCII-safe text from supported AI sites.
- Keep client-ready deliverables free from risky Unicode formatting.
- Detect smart quotes, em dashes, ellipses, invisible characters, bullets, arrows, and similar characters.
- Process text locally in the browser.

Avoid language like:

- Hide AI use.
- Bypass AI detection.
- Make AI text undetectable.
- Definitively detect AI writing.

Style Review is not AI detection. It only highlights phrases that may sound generic or over-polished and may deserve human review.
